About Me

A passionate story teller who uses data to weave compelling narratives.

My Journey

A little over four years ago I found myself in a position where I needed to work through vast amounts of data to make informed business decisions. I was responsible for managing the production of an entire customer fulfillment center and only knew the basics of analyzing and interpreting data. I quickly realized that to be successful in this role that data would become my most valuable asset. So I got to work. I started collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data so that it could illuminate the path to success. I began building dashboards, reports, and tools that allow me see beyond the numbers and chart a course for the future. Nearly half a decade later I am still on that journey. With every dataset charting the course into what was once unknown territory. Today, I may be unsure of what secrets the future holds, but I am confident that through careful planning and analyis that data will chart a course to the stars.
